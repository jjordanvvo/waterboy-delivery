Jug Deposit & Return Policy

How Deposits Work

Each 5-gallon jug carries a $5 refundable deposit. The deposit is charged when we deliver a new jug and credited back when the empty is returned — whether via swap, drop-off, or scheduled pickup.

  • Jug deposit: $5 per jug — fully refundable
  • Deposits are tracked on your account automatically
  • If a jug is lost or damaged, the deposit is forfeited
  • Cancel your plan? We'll schedule a final pickup and refund all deposits
  • Credit can be applied to future orders or refunded to your payment method

Questions about your deposits? Contact us or call (916) 619-3218.
