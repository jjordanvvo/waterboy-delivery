What Is It

What Is Alkaline Water?

Alkaline water has a higher pH level than regular purified water — ours measures pH 8.5 or above, compared to the neutral pH 7.0 of standard purified water. This is achieved through a careful ionization and mineral-balancing process.

Many customers report that alkaline water has a noticeably smoother, cleaner taste — particularly compared to tap water or even standard purified water. Athletes, fitness enthusiasts, and health-conscious families have made it their go-to daily hydration choice.
