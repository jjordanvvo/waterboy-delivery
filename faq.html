    FAQ.

    Everything you need to know about Water Boy Delivery — pricing, scheduling, water types, and more.

    What types of water do you deliver?

    We deliver three types: RO (reverse osmosis) water — pure, filtered, neutral taste. Alkaline water — pH 8.5+, with added trace minerals for a smooth, slightly mineral taste. Hydrogen water — infused with molecular hydrogen, our premium option.

    How much does delivery cost?

    Delivery is free for Zone 1 (within 3 miles of our store at 7119 Elk Grove Blvd). Zone 2 (3–6 miles) is $4.99 per delivery. Zone 3 (6–9 miles) is $9.99 per delivery. Call us to confirm your zone.

    What container sizes do you offer?

    We offer 1-gallon, 2-gallon, 3-gallon, and 5-Gallon bottles. The 5-Gallon bottle is our most popular option for home and office delivery. All bottles are food-safe, BPA-free polycarbonate.

    Do I need to sign a contract?

    No. We don't require contracts or minimum orders. You can schedule a one-time delivery or set up recurring service — weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly. Cancel or adjust anytime.

    How do I cancel or pause my delivery?

    Just call us at (916) 619-3218 or send a message through the contact page. We ask for 24 hours notice to skip or cancel a scheduled delivery. No cancellation fees.

    How does bottle pickup work?

    Leave your empty bottles at the door on delivery day. Our driver picks them up and replaces them with full ones. You don't need to be home. A small refundable deposit is collected on your first order to cover the bottles.

    What days do you deliver?

    We deliver Monday through Saturday. You'll receive a reminder notification the evening before your delivery, and a notification when the driver is on the way. Exact delivery windows vary by zone and day.

    Do I need a water dispenser?

    Not necessarily — you can use a ceramic crock or place the bottle directly in a standard top-load dispenser. If you need a dispenser, we sell and rent Brio models starting at $129.99 to purchase or $10/month to rent.

    How is the water filtered?

    All our water goes through a multi-stage process: sediment pre-filtration, activated carbon block filtration, reverse osmosis membrane, post-carbon polishing, and UV sterilization. Alkaline water has an additional ionization stage. See our Water Filtration page for full details.

    Do you deliver to my neighborhood?

    We serve Elk Grove, Sacramento, Rancho Cordova, Wilton, Galt, Florin, and surrounding Sacramento County areas. Check our Delivery Areas page or call us to confirm availability at your address.

    Still Have Questions?

    We'll answer them.

    Call us Monday through Saturday, 8 AM to 6 PM — or send a message and we'll respond same day.
