Our Story

About Waterboy Delivery

Local. Reliable. Affordable. We're Elk Grove's own water delivery service — built on the belief that everyone deserves great water without the national-brand markup.

Who We Are

Elk Grove's Local Water Delivery

Waterboy Delivery was founded with a simple idea: Elk Grove deserves a better water delivery option. One that's local, affordable, and actually shows up when it says it will.

We operate out of our store at 7119 Elk Grove Blvd and personally handle deliveries to homes, offices, and businesses throughout Sacramento County. No call centers, no corporate bureaucracy — just us, your water, and a text when we're on the way.

We're also the only local delivery service that carries alkaline pH 8.5+ water — something the big guys don't offer at all.
