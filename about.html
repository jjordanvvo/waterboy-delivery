    About Us.

    Family-owned water delivery based in Elk Grove, CA. Clean water, honest prices, neighbors serving neighbors.

    Who We Are

    Built in Elk Grove.
    For Elk Grove.

    Water Boy Delivery LLC was founded with a simple goal: give Elk Grove families and businesses access to clean, affordable water without the hassle of big-box stores or overpriced national delivery services.

    We're a local company — not a franchise, not a call center. Every delivery is handled by people who live and work in Sacramento County. When you call, you talk to someone who knows your neighborhood.

    We deliver RO water, alkaline water (pH 8.5+), and hydrogen water in multiple container sizes to homes and offices across Elk Grove, Sacramento, and surrounding areas. No contracts. Cancel anytime.
