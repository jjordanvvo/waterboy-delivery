Simple & Easy

How Waterboy Delivery Works

Four steps from sign-up to fresh water on your doorstep. No contracts, no hassle, no lugging heavy jugs.

The Process

Getting Started Is Easy

1

Choose Your Plan

Pick your water type (regular purified or alkaline pH 8.5+), select how many 5-gallon jugs you need per delivery, and choose your frequency — monthly, bi-weekly, or weekly. Plans start at $21/month with no contracts.

2

We Confirm & Schedule

After signing up, we'll reach out by call or text within one business day to confirm your first delivery date and time window. Deliveries run Monday through Saturday, 8 AM to 6 PM.

3

Fresh Jugs Arrive at Your Door

We bring your full jugs directly to you. When we deliver, we collect your empty jugs at the same time — automatic swap, no separate pickup needed. You're never left with a pile of empties.

4

Repeat on Your Schedule

We keep coming back on your agreed schedule. Need to skip a delivery? Add an extra jug? Change your frequency? Text us anytime — we're flexible, and there are never any cancellation fees.

Quick Answers

Common Questions

Do I need to be home for delivery?

Not necessarily. Many customers leave their empty jugs on the porch and we swap them out and leave the full ones. Just let us know your preference when you sign up.

What if I need to skip a delivery?

Just text or call us before your scheduled delivery date. No fees, no paperwork — we'll push your next delivery to the following cycle.

How do jug deposits work?

Each jug has a $5 refundable deposit. We charge it when we deliver a new jug and credit it back when the empty is returned. See our Bottle Pickup page for full details.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. There are no contracts or cancellation fees. Cancel with a text or call and we'll stop future deliveries and refund any outstanding deposits.
