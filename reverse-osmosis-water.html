    What it is

    Ultra-Pure Filtration.

    Reverse osmosis forces water through a semi-permeable membrane, removing up to 99% of dissolved solids, chlorine, fluoride, heavy metals, and other contaminants. What's left is exceptionally clean, neutral water.

    RO water is the baseline — pure and tasteless. It's ideal for drinking, cooking, baby formula, coffee, and anyone who is sensitive to chlorine taste or wants the lowest possible TDS count.
