FAQ

Questions? We've Got Answers.

Everything you need to know about water delivery and dispensers.

How does water delivery work?

It's simple — choose your plan, tell us how many 5-gallon jugs you need, and we deliver on your schedule. We also pick up your empty jugs each time. You never have to think about it.

What kind of water do you deliver?

We deliver multi-stage purified drinking water in BPA-free 5-gallon jugs. Our water goes through reverse osmosis, carbon filtration, and UV treatment to ensure the highest quality.

Do I need to buy a dispenser?

No — you can rent one from us (rental plans coming soon), buy one outright, or use your own existing dispenser. Any standard top-load or bottom-load cooler works with our 5-gallon jugs.

Can I change or cancel my plan?

Absolutely. All plans are month-to-month with no contracts. You can change your delivery frequency, add or remove jugs, pause for vacation, or cancel anytime — no penalties.

Do you deliver to businesses?

Yes! We serve homes, offices, gyms, clinics, salons, and any business that wants clean, cold water for their team or customers. Our Business plan includes custom scheduling and a dedicated account manager.

What areas do you serve?

We serve Elk Grove, Sacramento, and the greater Sacramento County area. Call us at (916) 619-3218 to confirm delivery to your address.

How much does delivery cost?

Delivery is always free. It's included in every plan. No delivery surcharges, no fuel fees, no surprise costs.

What brands of dispensers do you sell?

We currently carry Brio water dispensers — one of the top-rated brands on the market. We offer bottom-load, top-load, and bottleless filtration models. Contact us for current pricing and availability.
