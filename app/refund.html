How to Request a Refund

If you are not satisfied with an order, contact us as soon as possible after delivery. We review every complaint and work to make it right.

  • Call us: (916) 753-3866 (Monday through Saturday)
  • Email us: info@elkgrovewaterboy.com

Please have your order number ready when you contact us. We process all refund requests within 3 to 5 business days.

Bottle Return and Deposit Refund

Empty bottles are picked up by your driver on your next scheduled delivery. You do not need to take any action beyond placing empty bottles at your delivery location.

  • Bottle deposits are refunded automatically when bottles are returned in good condition.
  • If you cancel your service, your driver will schedule a pickup for any bottles you still have. Your deposit will be refunded within 5 to 7 business days after pickup.
  • Bottles must be empty and returned with the original cap in place.
  • Bottles with cracks, severe scuffs, or contamination that prevents safe refilling will not receive a full deposit refund.

Dispenser Return Policy

For customers renting a water dispenser, returns are handled as follows:

  • Contact us to schedule a dispenser pickup. We will coordinate a time with you at no charge.
  • The dispenser must be in clean, working condition to receive a full security deposit refund.
  • Normal wear and tear is expected and will not affect your deposit refund.
  • Damage beyond normal use, including cracks, broken components, or modification of the unit, may result in a partial or no deposit refund.
  • Security deposit refunds are processed within 7 business days of pickup and inspection.

See the Rental Agreement for full dispenser rental terms.

What Is Not Refundable

The following items are not eligible for refunds:

  • Water that has already been consumed
  • Bottle deposits for bottles damaged due to customer mishandling
  • Delivery fees for successfully completed deliveries
  • Orders where the customer was unavailable and the order was rescheduled (rescheduling fees may apply for repeated occurrences)

If you believe a refund should be issued in a situation not covered above, please contact us directly. We consider every situation on a case-by-case basis.
