Who We Are

Waterboy Delivery is a water delivery business located at 7119 Elk Grove Blvd, Elk Grove, CA 95758. We serve Elk Grove and surrounding areas in Sacramento County. You can reach us at (916) 753-3866.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights under California law, including the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

Information We Collect

When you create an account or place an order, we collect:

  • Full name
  • Delivery address
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Payment information (processed securely through Stripe; we do not store full card numbers)
  • Order and delivery history
  • Delivery preferences and notes (such as gate codes or drop locations)

When you visit our website, we may also collect:

  • Browser type and device information
  • Pages visited and time spent on the site
  • IP address and general location (city or region)
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Processing and fulfilling your water delivery orders
  • Scheduling and coordinating delivery appointments
  • Communicating with you about your orders, account, and deliveries
  • Sending delivery confirmations, receipts, and service updates
  • Improving our delivery routes and service quality
  • Responding to your questions or support requests
  • Sending promotional offers, if you have opted in
  • Complying with applicable laws and regulations

We will not use your personal information for any purpose not described in this policy without your consent.

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Payment processing: We use Stripe to process payments. Stripe receives your payment card information to complete transactions. Stripe is PCI DSS Level 1 certified. We do not store your full card number.
  • Delivery operations: Our drivers receive your name, delivery address, and relevant delivery notes to fulfill your order. This information is used only for delivery purposes.
  • Legal compliance: We may share information if required by law, court order, or government authority.

We do not share your personal information with advertisers or data brokers.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience. Cookies are small text files stored in your browser. We use them to:

  • Keep you signed in to your account
  • Remember your preferences and cart contents
  • Understand how visitors use our site so we can improve it
  • Measure the effectiveness of our marketing

You can accept or decline cookies using the banner that appears when you first visit the site. If you decline, we will not set non-essential tracking cookies. You can also clear cookies at any time through your browser settings.

Your California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:

  • Right to Know: You have the right to request a list of the personal information we have collected about you, where it came from, and how we use it.
  • Right to Delete: You have the right to request that we delete the personal information we have collected about you, subject to certain exceptions required by law or for completing your active orders.
  • Right to Opt Out of Sale: We do not sell your personal information. You do not need to take any action to opt out.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your privacy rights. Exercising these rights will not affect your ability to use our service.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the information below. We will respond within 45 days as required by California law.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. We keep order history and delivery records for up to 3 years after the last order for accounting and service quality purposes. Payment records are retained as required by law.

If you close your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal information within 90 days, except for records we are required to retain by law.

Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information, including encrypted connections (HTTPS), tokenized payment processing through Stripe, and restricted access to customer data. While no system is completely secure, we take reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.
