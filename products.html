Reverse Osmosis Water

6-stage purified water — the gold standard for drinking water purity. Bring any clean container 1–5 gallons.

  • Stage 1: Sediment pre-filter
  • Stage 2–3: Activated carbon
  • Stage 4: RO membrane (removes 99%+ contaminants)
  • Stage 5: Post-carbon polish
  • Stage 6: UV sterilization

Alkaline Water

Same 6-stage RO process, then remineralized with natural minerals for a smooth, clean taste and elevated pH.

  • pH ~9.5 — elevated alkalinity
  • Calcium & magnesium added back
  • Potassium for electrolyte balance
  • Smoother, sweeter taste vs. plain RO
  • Hydrates more efficiently

Hydrogen Water
