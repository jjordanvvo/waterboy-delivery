What It Is

Infused with Molecular Hydrogen.

Hydrogen water is purified water infused with extra molecular hydrogen (H2) gas. The dissolved hydrogen is what sets it apart from standard drinking water, and it gives the water a clean, light taste with no aftertaste.

Molecular hydrogen is valued for its antioxidant properties, which is why hydrogen water has become popular with athletes and active people. Many drink it before or after exercise as part of a daily hydration routine, where it may support how refreshed you feel.

It starts from the same purified base as our other water and is delivered fresh in reusable bottles. It is a good fit if you lead an active lifestyle and want something beyond standard drinking water.
